# Eventify

**Eventify** is a scientific events discovery platform dedicated to Algerian universities, designed to connect students, researchers, professors, and scientific clubs with academic opportunities nationwide. Our platform centralizes conferences, workshops, hackathons, club activities, seminars, and competitions, making event discovery, participation, and management effortless and engaging.

## Executive Summary

Many university students in Algeria miss valuable scientific events due to fragmented and unorganized sources of information. **Eventify** solves this by providing a unified, professional, and accessible hub—personalized by field, university, and interests. Our mission is to boost participation, collaboration, and innovation in Algerian academia and beyond.

**Target Audience:** University students, professors, researchers, and scientific clubs, primarily within scientific and technological fields in Algeria.

**Vision:** To become the primary academic event network in Algeria and expand regionally to support North African research collaboration.

---

## Features

- **Event Discovery:** Browse and filter upcoming academic and scientific events by category, date, and location.
- **Personalized Calendar Integration:** Add selected events directly to your device’s calendar.
- **Favorites & Notifications:** Save interesting events and get reminders.
- **Organizer Portal:** Verified universities and clubs can publish and manage their events.
- **Map View:** Visualize nearby events based on location.
- **Discussion Board:** Engage with other attendees, share reviews, and discuss events.
- **Recommendation System:** Get smart suggestions based on your interests and engagement history.
- **Premium Features:** Enhanced event promotion and visibility for organizers, badges, and sponsored placements.

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** Flutter (Dart) for mobile development
- **Backend:** Flask or Firebase (user profiles, authentication, events management)
- **Database:** Cloud-hosted solution, scalable for national access
- **DevOps:** Continuous integration and deployment for rapid updates
- **Other Tools:** Figma for design, third-party API integration for calendar and map features

## Business & Revenue Model

- **Freemium:** Free for browsing and attending events; premium options for organizers.
- **Subscriptions:** Verified clubs and institutions can subscribe for advanced engagement tools.
- **Advertisements:** Non-intrusive, science-focused ads; CPC & CPM models.
- **One-time Purchases:** Badges, featured event boosts, and promotional banners.
- **Sponsorships & Partnerships:** Strategic collaboration with universities and tech organizations.
- **Data Monetization:** Only aggregate, anonymized engagement data (user privacy strictly protected).

## Roadmap and Milestones

- **Beta Launch:** Select universities, pilot testing, and UI refinement
- **Soft Launch:** Regional roll-out, expand user base
- **Full Launch:** National expansion, public release, app stores campaign
- **Feature Updates:** Quarterly based on user feedback, including recommendation algorithms and retention strategies
- **User Retention:** Notifications, updates, newsletters, badges, and engagement points
